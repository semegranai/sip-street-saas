Overview

Sip Steet is an internal financial modeling platform designed to replace Excel and Google Sheets with a fast, standardized, and reliable system for underwriting real estate development deals.

The goal is simple:

Model a deal in minutes.
Trust the numbers.
Produce investor-ready output instantly.

Problem

Current workflow (Excel-based) creates friction:

Models break easily
Versions get lost
Calculations are inconsistent
Time per deal is too high
Outputs are not investor-friendly
Difficult to scale across team members
Objective

Build a web-based platform that:

Standardizes all deal modeling
Eliminates calculation inconsistency
Reduces time to underwrite a deal to <10 minutes
Produces clean, consistent, investor-ready reports
Serves as a foundation for multi-asset expansion
